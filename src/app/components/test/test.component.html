ABOUT ET CAMPUS CODING STARS POWERED BY BRIDGELABZ

“ET Campus Coding Stars powered by BridgeLabz” is a talent assessment program devised to hunt for the most talented coding minds in India. The contest involves an MCQ formatted test which would evaluate the coding proficiency of the participants. Candidates would require around 15 minutes to complete the test.

BENEFITS


Job Assistance For Coding Proficient Talent


Recognition For Top Coding Minds


Grooming For Top Technology Jobs

HOW TO CRACK IT ?

To prepare for the test, participants can begin with solving the most common programming exercises and further practice the basic elements of coding that are part of their curriculum. Participants are also advised to brush up their knowledge on subjects such as Data Structures, Algorithms (i.e. sorting, searching, etc.). Your performance in ET Campus Coding Stars will only determine whether you qualify to be in our list of the brightest coding minds in India or not and it is an individual test acting as an incentive for your benefit. Your performance in this test will have no bearing on your ETCS journey.
